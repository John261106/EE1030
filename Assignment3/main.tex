\solution The length of a vector is defined as\\
\begin{align}
	\norm{x} &= \sqrt{x^Tx}\\
	A &=\myvec{-6\\8} \\
	A^TA &=\myvec{-6, 8}\myvec{-6\\8} = 6^2=8^2=100\\
	\norm{A} &= \sqrt{100}=10
\end{align}
$\therefore$ Length of line segment $OA$ is $10$ units
